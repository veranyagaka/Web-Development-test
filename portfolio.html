About

I am a medical doctor, specializing in global health. With 5 years of experience, I have a strong background in policy and advocacy. My journey in healthcare has been driven by a commitment to improving patient outcomes and enhancing healthcare systems.

Currently, I am working at the WHO Kenya Office, where I am in chatge of strategic partnerships and collaboration among key stakeholders in the health space. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Medicine and Surgery from Kenyatta University, and I continuously seek opportunities to stay at the forefront of medical advancements.
